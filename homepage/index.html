Engineers.MY

Engineers.MY aims to support the Malaysian technology scene via

  • Connecting People. Encourage discovery of technology user groups and communication between each others.
  • Sharing Knowledge Learning, mentor and share advices. Reduce technology gap between the novice and the expert.
  • Collaboration Find like-minded people to work together on FOSS projects, community building and commercially.
